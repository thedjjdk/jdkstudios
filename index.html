The Person Behind It

DJ JDK

It started in fourth grade — a kid in percussion ensemble who didn't yet know what music would become to him. By late seventh grade, something clicked. By high school, there was no turning back. Since then, music hasn't just been a career; it's been a way of life — in school, outside of it, everywhere in between.

The journey started in rap and hip-hop, but 2024 changed that. Rock, country, and more have entered the picture — and that expansion isn't stopping. Every genre is a new lens, a new set of tools, a new way to hear the world.

Whatever the discipline — mixing a live show, engineering a record, shooting a set, designing a frame — the approach never changes: passionate, driven, and obsessively detail-oriented.
